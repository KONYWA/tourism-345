Arusha National Park Day Trip

Overview:

Despite the small size of Arusha National Park, common animals include giraffe, buffalo, zebra, warthog, the black-and-white colobus monkey, the blue monkey, flamingos, and more. African elephants and leopards are present, but rarely seen. The park is at the base of Mount Meru, which is good for climbing. Bird-life in the forest is prolific, with many forest species more easily seen here.

Day Description:

‚Ä¢ A transfer from the airport can be arranged for an extra cost.

Day 1: The morning after breakfast, our driver will pick you up from the hotel and drive to Arusha National Park to start the game drive. Despite the small size of the park, common animals include giraffes, buffalo, zebra, warthog, the black-and-white colobus monkey, blue monkey, flamingos, and more. African elephants and leopards are present but rarely seen. The park is at the base of Mount Meru, which is good for climbing, and walking safari is possible here. Birdlife in the forest is prolific, with many forest species more easily seen here. Although very accessible, Arusha National Park isn't on many safari itineraries due to the absence of the Big 5. The park has a range of habitats including forest, home to the black-and-white colobus monkey, and is excellent for birding. After a whole day of game drive, we will drive you back to your accommodation in Arusha.
