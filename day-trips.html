Arusha National Park Day Trip

Overview:

Arusha National Park is a great choice for a day trip, offering diverse wildlife and breathtaking views. Located at the base of Mount Meru, itâ€™s a haven for nature lovers and wildlife enthusiasts.

Location:

Arusha, Tanzania. Easily accessible from Arusha city.

Day Description:

After breakfast, you will be picked up and driven to Arusha National Park for a full-day game drive. The park is home to giraffes, zebras, buffaloes, flamingos, and the elusive leopards and elephants. You will also have a chance to explore Mount Meru's base and enjoy a walking safari.

Activities:

  • Game drive through Arusha National Park
  • Bird watching (many species are visible in the park)
  • Optional walking safari at the base of Mount Meru

Meals & Drinks:

  • Lunch included
  • Non-alcoholic drinks included
  • Breakfast and dinner not included

Rates:

$495 per person

Included in the Price:

  • Park fees (for non-residents)
  • All activities as per itinerary
  • Transport in a 4x4 vehicle
  • Lunch
  • Drinks (non-alcoholic)

Excluded from the Price:

  • International flights
  • Roundtrip airport transfer
  • Additional accommodation
