1. NGORONGORO CRATER DAY TRIP

Overview

Ngorongoro Crater, known as the "Eighth Wonder of the World," is one of the most spectacular wildlife destinations in Africa. It is home to a variety of animals, including lions, rhinos, elephants, zebras, and wildebeests, and offers unparalleled wildlife viewing. The crater floor is also rich in birdlife.

2. TARANGIRE NATIONAL PARK DAY TRIP

Overview

Tarangire National Park is known for its large herds of elephants, Baobab trees, and the vast landscape. It offers a quieter alternative to the more popular Serengeti and Ngorongoro Crater with a chance to see lions, leopards, buffalo, zebras, and giraffes.

Day Description

  • A transfer from the airport can be arranged for an extra cost.

Day 1

After breakfast, our driver will pick you up and drive you to Tarangire National Park. Spend the day exploring the park's diverse landscapes, from the riverbanks to the open savannahs. Enjoy game drives, where you'll encounter herds of elephants, lions, and other wildlife. After a full day of wildlife encounters, we will drive you back to your accommodation.
