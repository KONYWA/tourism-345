Overview

You will be picked up from your hotel in Arusha between 6AM to 7AM and starting drive to Ngorongoro Crater and pass through the crater viewpoint, where you will descend into the crater for a game drive. The Ngorongoro Crater is often referred to as the Eighth Natural Wonder of the World and is the largest intact crater in the world. Formed over 2 million years ago when the Ngorongoro volcano erupted, the crater walls collapsed to create a natural enclosure. The crater, now over 19 kilometers wide, is home to a diverse range of habitats including acacia forests, swamps filled with hippos, and open grasslands. With over 30,000 animals, including elephants, warthogs, flamingoes, and the rare black rhinoceros, as well as a variety of birdlife and predatory cats, the crater offers a rich wildlife viewing experience. Later after the game drive, you will be dropped off at your hotel or airport. Meals
